About Me

Hello! I'm Priti, a passionate web developer and UI designer with a background in Computer Science Engineering. I currently work at Ayodhya Webosoft, where I bring digital ideas to life and craft exceptional user experiences. I've had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects. From e-commerce websites that simplify online shopping to interactive web applications that engage users, my work reflects my commitment to innovation and excellence.

I believe that a well designed website should not only look visually appealing but a smooth and enjoyable interaction for visitors. When starting a new project, I throughly research and analyze the target audience and the client's specific requirements. this enables me to tailor my desgins to meet their expectations and deliver a unique online presence that aligns with their brand identity. I offer a range of services, including website design and development, UI/UX design, and front-end optimization, all rooted in my Computer Science knowledge.
